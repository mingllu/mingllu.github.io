About Dr. M. L. Lu

Overhead transmission line design is hardly taught at colleagues and universities. Engineers and designers often have to learn on jobs and mostly resort to industry standards for guidance. This website is dedicated to an all-in-one electronic handbook, aiming to cover all aspects of overhead power transmission line design in a convenient, user-friendly, transparent and thorough manner, including basic concepts, basic theories, basic design tools, as well as in-depth special topics. It can serve as a textbook, a handbook, and a reference book at the same time.
